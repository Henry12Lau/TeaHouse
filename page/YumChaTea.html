Yum Cha Tea Company Shares Its Love of Tea and Nature

Yum Cha Tea Company, a popular tea brand out of Jacksonville, Fla., is focused on being at the forefront of holistic wellness while supporting the bee population.

The company is home to more than 250 teas – including functional teas – and the business encourages tea lovers to create their own tea experience that caters to overall wellness and comfort, while boosting immune systems and mental and physical health.

As part of its mission, the brand uses a portion of its proceeds to create beehives or bee yards, which the company plants to help save bees.
